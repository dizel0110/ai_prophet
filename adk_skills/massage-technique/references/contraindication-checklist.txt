ABSOLUTE CONTRAINDICATIONS:
- Acute thrombosis, phlebitis
- Contagious diseases, infections
- Severe osteoporosis
- Recent fracture or surgery (< 6 weeks)
- Malignant tumors at massage site
- Acute rheumatoid arthritis flare
- Severe cardiovascular conditions

LOCAL CONTRAINDICATIONS:
- Varicose veins
- Bruises, cuts, inflammation
- Sunburn, rashes, skin infections
- Hematomas
- Recent scars (< 6 months)
