Common Pain Patterns:

LOWER BACK:
- Muscle strain: dull ache, worse with movement
- Disc issues: sharp pain, may radiate to leg
- Piriformis syndrome: deep gluteal pain, sciatica-like
- Facet joint: pain worse with extension

NECK & SHOULDER:
- Upper trapezius: tension headaches, neck stiffness
- Levator scapulae: pain at shoulder angle, limited rotation
- Rotator cuff: deep shoulder pain, weakness

HEAD & JAW:
- Tension headache: band-like pressure
- TMJ: jaw clicking, facial pain
- Cervicogenic: from neck, one-sided
